The Brief

Qudini required a website that supported their growing need to increase awareness of their alternative software solutions. The making pretty team needed to work incrdibly closely with the client.

The Solution

The sites numerous user journeys elevated the importance of solving the UX before proceeding into design and this was done in meticulous detail. With the breadth of material the site needed to be digestible and easily navigable, using bespoke design elements, the potential for monotony was prevented and instead a unique, enjoyable experience was created for the user.
