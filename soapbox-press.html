The Brief

Soapbox Press is an independent publishing house and platform for the newly established creative voice. Soapbox’s aim is to assist students and graduates in the transition from university into industry.

The Solution

The platform represents many young creatives’ through interviews, visuals and writing, it aims to prove the opportunity to be showcased and receive publicity.
