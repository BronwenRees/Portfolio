The Brief

In 1989, Rufus Leonard was born; an agency that over the last 25 years has delivered many firsts.To celebrate this occasion, a digital timeline was created.

The Solution

Initially designed for desktop and tablet devices, the intricate details were too complex for mobile and so a responsive and simpler version now supports the desktop site. Users can read the story as a whole or can jump to a specific year through the navigation bar.
