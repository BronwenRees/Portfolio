The Brief

The Graduate is a startup platform aimed at helping graduates transition from a university life to a working one. The Graduate required a complete a brand and identity, alongside a fully responsive website.

The Solution

Covering in depth and difficult information, the brand remains engaging through the use of colour and illustration. The complexity of the site requires a carefully considered user experience and features such as breadcrumb navigation, progress bars and easy search functions. How the user engages with the information will depict the information presented to them.
